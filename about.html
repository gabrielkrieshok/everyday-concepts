Everyday Concepts are an ongoing effort to unpack and explore useful concepts, ideas, and mental models that crop up (or ought to) in our lives.

I am working on a book of these concepts that further explore how to visualize them and their relevance to everyday life.

Additionally, I am developing a weekly podcast with special guests that explore one concept in more depth. Follow me at @gabrielkrieshok to stay tuned.

Defined, sketched, and produced by me, Gabriel Krieshok.
